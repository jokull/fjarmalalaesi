# Practical Moves — Verified, Quantified, Conditional (July 2026)

Concrete money moves that survive scrutiny. **Presentation rules for
agents** (this is what separates knowledge from grift):

1. Every claim here carries a number, a condition, and a verify-by source —
   repeat all three when relaying, never just the headline.
2. Compute the value **for the user's own numbers** before recommending
   (a 2pp FX saving is 200 kr on a 10k purchase and 40k on a 2M trip —
   only one of those deserves a mention).
3. These are July 2026 facts with expiry dates. If the source hasn't been
   re-checked this calendar year, say so.
4. Never present as "hacks" or secrets — everything here is public,
   intended-use policy.

## Cards & daily money

- **Foreign purchases (online and abroad) through indó debit**: 0.7% FX
  markup vs 2.3–2.7% at the big banks — ~2pp saved, ≈20,000 kr per 1M kr
  of foreign spend. Was 0% until June 24 2026; re-check indo.is/verdskra.
- **Under 23–24?** The big banks waive debit-card árgjald and transaction
  fees — if a young person is paying card fees, they're at the wrong tier.
- **ATM abroad**: Landsbankinn debit 1.0% is the cheapest of the banks;
  credit-card ATM withdrawals cost 2.75% + min-fee everywhere — avoid.

## Salary & employer money

- **Capture the 2% séreign match** — conditional on contributing ≥2%
  yourself; it's a 100% instant return (10,000 kr/mo free at 500k gross).
  The single highest-ROI checkbox in Icelandic personal finance.
- **Starfsmenntasjóður**: 90% of course costs, up to ~180,000 kr/yr,
  accumulating to ~540,000 over 3 years (VR/SVS, Efling/Starfsafl) —
  chronically unclaimed. Any course, certification, or even driving
  licenses at some funds; check your union's rules.
- **VR members: varasjóður** — a personal account (~4% of one month's
  salary deposited yearly) spendable on dental, glasses, gym, psychology.
  It carries over; many members don't know it exists.
- **Ask for tax-free fringes, not taxable ones**: íþróttastyrkur ≤85,000/yr
  and samgöngustyrkur ≤138,000/yr (green commute) are tax-free — worth
  nearly 2× their face value vs the same amount as top-bracket salary.
  A company car is the opposite: 28%/yr of purchase price TAXABLE
  (a 10M car ≈ 1.17M kr/yr in real tax cost at the top bracket).
- **Time the launaviðtal for autumn** and agree personal raises explicitly
  "til viðbótar við kjarasamningsbundnar hækkanir" — otherwise the January
  contractual bump (+3.5% 2026/2027) can be silently absorbed into it.
- **Spouse persónuafsláttur**: 100% transferable — worth up to 72,492
  kr/mo when one spouse is studying, on leave, or low-income. Set it in
  the employer's payroll registration; refunds true up at álagning.

## Savings & tax

- **Split savings between spouses**: the 300,000 kr/yr frítekjumark on
  capital income is per person — a couple shelters interest on ~8.5M kr
  at current top deposit rates by holding accounts in both names.
- **Move cash off the veltureikningur**: default checking pays ~0–2%;
  óbundnir savings accounts pay 6.4–7.6% (Auður/Arion/Landsbankinn/indó
  baukar, July 2026). Same-day access, zero cost to switch.
- **Kill 15.5% debt before saving at 7%**: overdraft, credit-card
  revolving, and BNPL (17–40%+ effective) always lose to any savings
  plan. Only exception: a minimal emergency buffer first.
- **Fund units are tax-deferred**: taxed only at redemption (as interest,
  inside the frítekjumark pot) — accumulating funds beat interest-bearing
  accounts for money you won't touch for years, before even counting
  returns.

## Mortgages

- **1,000,000 kr/yr fee-free prepayment** even on fixed-rate loans at all
  three big banks (variable-rate loans: always free). Effective return =
  real rate + inflation, tax-free (~10%+ nominal at July 2026 levels).
- **Arion 3-yr verðtryggð resets**: free full exit in the last 90 days of
  each fixed period — diarize it; it's the refinancing window.
- **Refinancing friction is only ~80–100k and no stimpilgjald** — 0.25pp
  off an 80M loan saves 200k/yr. Check pension-fund rates (0.5–1pp below
  banks) once the balance is under ~65–70% of fasteignamat.
- **Séreign → mortgage redirect (time-sensitive)**: applications open
  Oct 1 2026 at Skatturinn; filing **before Dec 1 2026** sweeps
  contributions retroactively from Jan 1 2026. Up to 500,000 kr/yr per
  person, tax-free, per individual (couples 1M combined).

## Family

- **Reykjavík leikskóli: first 36 h/week free from April 2026** — a
  family previously paying full fees keeps 30–50k/mo. Frístundakort
  (75,000 kr/yr per child 6–18) expires unused if not claimed.
- **Fæðingarorlof planning**: the 80% is capped at 900,000/mo (binds
  above 1,125,000 gross), and the reference window ends 6 months before
  the birth month — recent raises are partly invisible. Cap changes now
  apply to leave already granted (Dec 2025 law), so deferring leave into
  a higher-cap year captures the increase.
- **Parental down-payment help**: fyrirframgreiddur arfur costs 10% flat;
  an informal large "gift" is legally taxable income for the recipient at
  up to 46.29%. The formal route through sýslumaður is almost always
  right for house-deposit-sized amounts.

## Safety nets

- **Unemployed? Have VMST deduct union dues from benefits** — it
  maintains sjúkrasjóður and fund rights for ~0.7% of a much smaller
  base. Losing them costs far more if illness follows joblessness.
- **Mitt Creditinfo is free** — see your own vanskilaskrá status, your
  lánshæfismat and its drivers, and who has been looking you up. Paid
  defaults are removed immediately on creditor confirmation; check before
  a mortgage application, not after the rejection.
- **Umboðsmaður skuldara is free** — including paying the 250,000 kr
  skiptatrygging for personal bankruptcy when the debtor can't. Nobody in
  payment trouble should pay for basic debt help.
- **Námslán niðurfelling is earned per semester** (20% on progress + 10%
  on on-time completion since Apr 2026) — dropping below progress
  requirements costs real money, not just time.
