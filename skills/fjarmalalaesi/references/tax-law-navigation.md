## 1. The statute map

Consolidated current law: **althingi.is/lagas/nuna/{YYYY}{NNN}.html**
(year + zero-padded law number, e.g. `2003090.html` = lög 90/2003).
⚠️ The consolidation reflects a dated Lagasafn edition (header says which —
currently April 2026); laws passed after that date are invisible there.

**Lög um tekjuskatt nr. 90/2003 — the master statute.** Internal geography:

| Article | Governs |
|---|---|
| 7. gr. | The three income classes: **A** wages/benefits/pensions, **B** business/self-employment, **C** capital (vextir, arður, söluhagnaður, leigutekjur). Classification determines everything downstream |
| 17. gr. | Söluhagnaður íbúðarhúsnæðis: 2-year rule, 600/1,200 m³ caps, rollover |
| 30. gr. | Deductions: pension contributions, ökutækjastyrkur/dagpeningar up to skattmat limits |
| 58. gr. | Reiknað endurgjald (mandatory minimum own-wage for ehf owners/self-employed) |
| **58. gr. a** | **The Airbnb pivot**: rental is business income by DEFAULT, except long-term húsaleigulög rental of ≤2 properties OR registered heimagisting within limits → then capital income |
| 62. gr. | Couples' pooled assessment (doubles the frítekjumark) |
| **66. gr.** | Skattstigar: wage brackets, the 22% capital rate, the 300k frítekjumark, the rental exempt share (25% from 2026), AND the automatic indexation rule (trailing CPI + 1.0% productivity add-on each Jan 1) |
| 67. gr. | Persónuafsláttur + spousal transferability |
| 68. gr. | Barnabætur (A) and vaxtabætur (B, winding down) |

## 2. The annual change cycle

1. **September**: fjárlagafrumvarp tabled with revenue companion bill.
2. **December**: the "bandormur" laws pass (two flavors: "vegna fjárlaga"
   and "um skatta og gjöld") — effective Jan 1. Example: lög 103/2025 cut
   the rental exempt share 50→25% for 2026.
3. **Bracket/persónuafsláttur indexation is automatic by formula** (66./67.
   gr.): trailing-12-month CPI + 1.0% productivity (five-yearly review).
   December announcements just compute it (2026: 4.5% + 1.0% = 5.5%).
4. **Late December**: the two canonical change-logs go live —
   - Ministry: "Skattabreytingar á árinu {X}" (stjornarradid.is) — best
     single summary of everything that changed
   - Skatturinn: "Skatthlutfall, skattþrep og persónuafsláttur ársins {X}"
     + `skatturinn.is/einstaklingar/helstutolur/{year}/`
   - RSK auglýsingar (skattmat, reiknað endurgjald) in Stjórnartíðindi B,
     listed at `skatturinn.is/fagadilar/auglysingar/{year}`
5. **By Dec 1**: each municipality fixes útsvar and fasteignagjöld
   multipliers for next year (álagningarforsendur).
6. **Mid-year changes are rare but real** — the June 2026 séreign law is
   the live example. They won't appear in helstutolur pages or lagas for
   months; only þingskjöl and Stjórnartíðindi A are timely.
7. **The álagning cycle** (separate): framtal ~March, álagning late
   May/June, refunds/bills from June 1. All deadlines: skattadagatal on
   skatturinn.is.

## 3. Interpretation sources

- **Skatturinn leiðbeiningar / Spurt og svarað** — per-domain plain-language
  rules with statute citations. Persuasive, not binding, and can lag laws.
- **Bindandi álit** (lög 91/1998): binding advance ruling on a *planned*
  (never completed) transaction; fee-based; appealable. Household
  use-cases: property-sale structuring near 17. gr. limits, rental
  conversion, family transfers.
- **Yfirskattanefnd** (yskn.is): the appeals tribunal — the definitive
  source on classification gray zones (gerviverktaka, rental-as-business,
  hlunnindi). Search: yskn.is/leit-i-urskurdum. Cite as "úrskurður
  yfirskattanefndar nr. N/YYYY". Appeal window: 3 months.
- Escalate past leiðbeiningar when: Airbnb straddles heimagisting limits,
  mixed personal/rental use near a sale, one-client contractor
  arrangements, non-cash benefits without a clean skattmat entry.

## 5. Verification workflow (run for ANY tax number)

1. **Current value**: `skatturinn.is/einstaklingar/helstutolur/{year}/`
   (works 2016–2026); brackets at `.../stadgreidsla/skattthrep/{year}/`;
   skattmat/reiknað endurgjald via `.../fagadilar/auglysingar/{year}`.
2. **Governing law**: `althingi.is/lagas/nuna/{YYYY}{NNN}.html` — check
   the header's Lagasafn edition date; if the topic may have moved since,
   sweep the December bandormar and "Nýlega samþykkt lög", and search
   island.is/stjornartidindi for promulgated text.
3. **Ambiguity**: yskn.is precedents → Skatturinn leiðbeiningar →
   bindandi álit for planned transactions.

Cross-check annual deltas against the ministry's "Skattabreytingar á
árinu {X}" post — one page, every change, with effective dates.

**Traps (all observed live):**
1. **Skatturinn pages lag new laws** — in July 2026 the séreign page still
   said the program "fell úr gildi" while Alþingi had made it permanent
   weeks earlier. A Skatturinn info page is never proof a scheme doesn't
   exist.
2. **Lagas consolidation lag** — anything passed after the edition date is
   invisible in `nuna` text.
3. **Bill numbers ≠ final law** — committees change provisions late (the
   séreign effective date and couple-cap both changed in committee). Only
   the "lög í heild" þingskjal or Stjórnartíðindi A is final.
4. English skatturinn.is pages lag the Icelandic ones.
5. Rental "rate" ambiguity: 22% × 75% = 16.5% effective — sources quote
   either; the exempt share flipped in 2026.
6. **Staðgreiðsla ≠ final tax**: withholding uses the national-average
   útsvar and per-employer brackets; álagning trues up.
7. Secondary sources (aggregators, KPMG/Deloitte booklets, Hagstofa APIs)
   are January snapshots — treat accordingly.
